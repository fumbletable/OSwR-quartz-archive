# Expert

**HD:** 6 + CON then 1d6 (3) + CON  
**Saves:** DEX, INT  
**Ability Boost:** +1 DEX  
**Weapons:** Light, Medium  
**Armor:** Light  

**Feats.** Gain 6 Expert Feats at level 1. At each odd level, +1 Expert Feat.  
(If you multiclass into Expert later, gain 3 when you take your first Expert level.)  

**Talent for Trouble.** If you start a scene in control (hidden, disguised, or with a plan), gain **EDGE** on your first check of that scene.  

**Boost Hook.** When you spend a Boost Die on a **DEX-based** attack/check, roll +1d6 and keep the higher Boost result.  

**Starting Kit (pick one):**  
- *Cutpurse:* Leather, Shortsword, Shortbow & 20 arrows, Thieves’ Tools, 2 Daggers.  
- *Face:* Leather, Rapier/Shortsword, Disguise Kit, Thieves’ Tools, 2 Daggers.
