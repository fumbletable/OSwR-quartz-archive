# Cleric

**HD:** 6 + CON then 1d6 (3) + CON  
**Saves:** WIS, CHA  
**Ability Boost:** +1 WIS  
**Weapons:** Light, Medium (Bludgeoning only)  
**Armor:** Light, Medium, Shields  

**Oath (pick one at 1st):**  
- *Hallowed Oath (Sanctity):* You gain access to the Cleric spell list (tiers 1–6) and the **Turn Undead** ability.  
- *Druidic Oath (Nature):* You gain access to the Druidic spell list (tiers 1–6). Druids do not turn undead. Their path instead opens unique Feats such as Shapechanging, Animal Empathy, and Woodland Mastery.  

**Spellcasting (SP).** WIS-based. Save DC = 8 + PB + WIS; Spell attack = PB + WIS.  
Prepare (level + WIS mod) spells; spend SP to cast; recover per [[01 Rules/Rests & Recovery]].  

**Boost Hook.** After a successful **Breather SP refresh**, you may spend 1 Boost Die to regain +1 SP.  

**Starting Kit (pick one):**  
- *War-Cleric:* Chain, Shield, Mace; Holy Symbol, 1 flask of consecrated water, Sling & 20 stones.  
- *Warden (Druidic):* Leather, Staff, Sling; Holy Symbol, Herbalist’s Kit.

### Turn Undead (Hallowed Oath only)

**Action:** Present Holy Symbol and call upon your power.

- Make a **WIS check (DC 12)**.  
- **On success:** affect `2d6 + PB` HD of undead within Near, starting with the lowest HD first.  
  - If the undead’s HD ≤ (your level − 3), it is **Destroyed**.  
  - If the undead’s HD ≤ (your level + 1), it is **Turned** for `2d6` rounds (must flee, can’t approach you).  
  - If its HD > (your level + 1), it is **unaffected**.  
- **On failure:** your attempt has no effect, and you cannot attempt Turn Undead again in this combat.
