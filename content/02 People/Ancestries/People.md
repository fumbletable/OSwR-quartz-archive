# Dwarf
**Size:** Medium · **Languages:** Common, Dwarvish

- **Senses — Darkvision (30 ft).**
- **Dwarven Resilience:** EDGE on saves vs **spells & spell-like effects**.
- **Stone Sense:** If you unknowingly pass within **5 ft** of unusual stonework (slopes, seams, stone traps, recent masonry), the Referee may roll a **secret INT** check for you. When you **actively** search stonework, roll with **EDGE**.
- **Stout Build:** When pushed or knocked, you have **EDGE** to resist being moved.

# Elf
**Size:** Medium · **Languages:** Common, Elvish

- **Keen Senses:** **EDGE** on sight/hearing checks; you can’t be **Surprised** by purely visual/audible threats at **NEAR** if alert.
- **Find Secret Doors:** Passing within **5 ft** of a secret/hidden door allows a **WIS** check (Referee may roll secretly). When you **actively** search, roll with **EDGE**.
- **Ghoulbane:** You ignore **paralysis from ghouls**.

# Halfling
**Size:** Small · **Languages:** Common, Halfling

- **Small & Quiet:** **EDGE** to **Hide** in wilderness; creatures **larger than you** have **SETBACK** to detect you beyond **CLOSE** if you’re still.
- **Halfling Luck:** **EDGE** on saves vs **fear** and morale-breaking effects.
- **Stout Heart:** When you would drop your weapon from shock/pain, you may **ignore it once per fight**.
> *Small has no extra rules—use gear tags like **Heavy/Bulky** elsewhere to gate items.*
