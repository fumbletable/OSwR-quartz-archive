# Build-a-People (variant recipe)

Pick **2–3 knobs** that fit the concept (don’t stack the same category twice):

- **Sense:** **Darkvision (30 ft)** *or* **EDGE** on (sight / hearing / smell) checks.
- **Resist:** **EDGE** on one save theme (**poison, charm, fear, spells, cold/heat**).
- **Knack:** **EDGE** on one exploration task (**track in marsh, find secret doors, stone sense, woodland stealth**).
- **Quirk:** small, always-on perk (**ignore first level of Winded once/day; treat Bulky items as 1 slot once per watch; “advantage-like moments” translate to EDGE**).

> Keep it terse; keep it table-useful.
