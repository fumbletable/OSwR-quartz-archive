# Fighter

**HD:** 8 + CON then 1d8 (4) + CON  
**Saves:** STR, CON  
**Ability Boost:** +1 STR  
**Weapons:** Light, Medium, Heavy  
**Armor:** Light, Medium, Heavy, Shields  

**Feats.** +1 Fighter Feat at each odd level.  

**First Strike.** On round 1, your first weapon attack gains **EDGE**.  

**Boost Hook.** On your turn, you may spend **1 Boost Die** to make **one additional attack**.  

**Starting Kit (pick one):**  
- *Shieldwall:* Chain, Shield, any Medium melee, Light Crossbow (30 bolts).  
- *Skirmisher:* Leather, Longbow (20 arrows), any **two** melee weapons.
