# Rests & Recovery

### Breather (10 minutes) — choose ONE
- **Heal:** Spend 1 Provision, regain `1d4 + CON` HP.  
- **Refresh SP (once/day):** Spend 1 Provision; roll INT (Magic-User) or WIS (Cleric), DC 12.  
  - On success: regain PB SP.  
  - You may spend 1 Boost Die to gain +1 SP on success.  
- **Clear Winded:** Spend 1 Provision and roll CON 12 (EDGE if gear/plan helps).  

You cannot combine these in the same Breather.

**Rally Breather (exception):**  
If someone dropped to 0 HP this fight, during the next Breather that hero may both **Heal** and **Clear Winded** if an adjacent ally spends **1 Provision** on them or the hero spends **2 Provisions**. (SP refresh still respects the once/day limit.)

---

### Night’s Rest (8 hours)
- Spend Hit Dice to heal.  
- Regain all SP; you may change prepared spells.  
- Fatigue: remove 2 if you had food and shelter; otherwise remove 1.  

---

### Safe Haven (1d4+1 days)
- **Full reset:** all HP, SP, and Fatigue restored.  
- **Injuries:** Minor clear automatically; Major require treatment or magic (Referee’s call).
