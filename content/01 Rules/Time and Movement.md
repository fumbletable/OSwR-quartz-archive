## Time and Movement

### Time Segments
- **Round:** 6 seconds (combat actions).  
- **Turn:** 10 minutes (dungeon exploration).  
- **Watch:** 6 hours (wilderness travel, marching, or rest).  
- **Day:** Full day of downtime, recovery, or overland travel.  

---

### Movement Speeds
- **Slow (15 ft/round):** cautious, sneaking, or encumbered.  
- **Normal (30 ft/round):** average pace.  
- **Fast (45 ft/round):** urgent or reckless movement.
