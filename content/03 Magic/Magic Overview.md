# Magic Overview

Magic in OSwR uses **spell slots**. Each time you cast a spell, you expend one slot of that tier or higher. Slots refresh after a **Night’s Rest** or at a **Safe Haven**.

- **Caster stat:** Cleric → WIS · Magic-User → INT  
- **Save DC:** 8 + PB + stat  
- **Spell attack:** PB + stat  
- **Focus:** Cleric → holy symbol · Magic-User → arcane focus  

---

## Preparing Spells

- After a **Night’s Rest** or at a **Safe Haven**, prepare spells equal to your **level + casting stat modifier**.  
- Preparing a spell adds it to your *list for the day*.  
- You may cast any spell from your prepared list, as long as you have an unspent slot of that tier or higher.  
- You don’t need to prepare the same spell multiple times. If *Magic Missile* is on your list, you can cast it as often as you have slots.  
- Changing your prepared list requires another rest.  

**Prep time:** Preparing spells takes **1 turn (10 minutes) × the highest spell level** you prepare.  
- Example: If you prepare only 1st-level spells, it takes 10 minutes.  
- If you prepare up to 2nd level, 20 minutes.  
- If you prepare up to 6th level, 60 minutes.  

| Highest Spell Level Prepared | Prep Time |
| ---------------------------- | --------- |
| 1st                          | 10 min    |
| 2nd                          | 20 min    |
| 3rd                          | 30 min    |
| 4th                          | 40 min    |
| 5th                          | 50 min    |
| 6th                          | 60 min    |

---

## Casting

- **Spend a slot** of the spell’s tier or higher when you cast. The slot is lost; the spell remains on your list.  
- Some spells are **Boostable**: you may spend a **Boost Die** to enhance the effect.  
  - Example: *Magic Missile* — add an extra dart.  
  - Example: *Cure Light Wounds* — heal an extra +1d4.  
- **Components:** No material components are tracked unless a spell specifies. All spells require **words and gestures**. You cannot cast if you are gagged, bound, or otherwise unable to speak/move freely.  
- **Concentration:** If a spell requires focus, taking damage forces a CON save (Normal 12) or the spell ends.  

---

## Recovery

- **Night’s Rest (8 hours):** regain all slots.  
- **Safe Haven (1d4+1 days):** full reset, all HP and slots.  
- **Breather (10 min):** does not restore slots, but some class Boost Hooks may interact here.

## Notes

- Clerics prepare from the **Cleric list** (or Druidic list if they took the Druidic Oath).  
- Magic-Users prepare from the **Arcane list**.  
- Rituals, scrolls, and magic items follow their own rules (see Magic Items).
