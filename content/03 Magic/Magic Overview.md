# Magic Overview

Magic in OSwR uses **spell slots**.  
Each time you cast a spell, you expend one slot of that spell’s level or higher.  
Slots refresh after a **Night’s Rest** or at a **Safe Haven**.

---

## Casting Basics
- **Caster stat:** Cleric → WIS · Magic-User → INT  
- **Save DC:** `8 + PB + caster stat`  
- **Spell attack:** `PB + caster stat`  
- **Focus:** Cleric → holy symbol  ·  Magic-User → arcane focus  

---

## Preparing Spells
- After a **Night’s Rest** or at a **Safe Haven**, prepare spells equal to  
  `level + caster stat modifier`.  
- Prepared spells form your *daily list*; you may cast any from that list while slots remain.  
- You don’t need to prepare duplicates — one prep allows multiple casts.  
- Changing your list requires another rest.  
- **Prep time:** `1 Turn (10 min) × highest spell level prepared.`  
  - 1st → 10 min  ·  2nd → 20 min  ·  3rd → 30 min  ·  4th → 40 min  ·  5th → 50 min  ·  6th → 60 min  

---

## Casting
- **Spend a slot** of that spell’s level or higher; the slot is lost, the spell remains prepared.  
- **Boostable:** You may spend a **Boost Die** to heighten a spell’s effect  
  (extra missile, extra healing, stronger duration, etc.).  
- **Components:** All spells require **words and gestures**.  
  You cannot cast if gagged, bound, or unable to move/speak freely.  
- **Concentration:** If maintaining a spell, damage forces a **CON save 12** or it ends.  

---

## Recovery
- **Breather (10 min):** No slot recovery.  
- **Night’s Rest (8 hr):** Restore all slots; may change prepared spells.  
- **Safe Haven (1d4 + 1 days):** Full reset — HP, slots, Fatigue.

## Notes
- Clerics prepare from the **Divine list**; Magic-Users prepare from the **Arcane list**.
- Druids prepare from the **Druidic list** (via Druidic Oath).
